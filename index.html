Who is King Sejong?

  • King Sejong was the fourth king of Joseon Dynasty, born on April 10, 1397, and died on February 17, 1450.
  • He is still the most beloved king among Koreans today because he loved the people and ruled the country with all his heart until his death.
  • He wrote and distributed books on farming to the people, but there were many people who could not read, so he made an easy-to-learn language.
  • He built a hall called Jiphyeonjeon inside the palace so that young scholars could concentrate on academic research. Through Jiphyeonjeon, many talented people were trained.
  • Excellent human resources have greatly improved the development of science and technology by creating many scientific inventions such as sundials, water clocks, and rain gauges.
